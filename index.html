    How to Play

    Objective

    Work together to maintain the Shared Model and hold the vision across 5 rounds of project challenges.

    Win Conditions

    • Vision Drift at 8 or below
    • At least 10 of 13 fields stable
    • Psychological Safety above 50%
    • Team can articulate Vision and Strategy

    Round Structure

    1. Draw Scenario Card - Context update for the week
    2. Draw Disruption Card - Challenge to the vision
    3. Play Viewpoint Cards - Respond from your perspective
    4. Negotiate - Discuss as a team
    5. Resolve - Vote on the response
    6. Update - Adjust trackers and tokens
    7. Replenish - Draw new Viewpoint Cards

    Strategic Pause

    When Vision Drift reaches 10, play stops. Use this time to reassess the Shared Model and restore stability.
